How It Works

From tap to
tracked in seconds.

01

Add your cards

Enter your card details — bank, limit, statement day, due date. Takes 60 seconds per card. No bank login required, ever.

02

Copy your API key

One click to copy your unique key from Settings. This authenticates your iPhone Shortcuts without any login flow.

03

Set up the Shortcut

Our Shortcut Helper walks you through every step. Paste your API key + card ID, add it to your home screen. Done in under 2 minutes.

04

Tap. Log. Done.

Every time you spend, tap your Shortcut. Enter the amount and merchant. CreditLens receives it instantly, updates your balance, and checks your budgets.
